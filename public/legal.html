Privacy Policy

RadioDock (radiodock.app) is operated by the person named above and hosted on GitHub Pages. The application itself does not collect personal data. Your favourites and custom station lists are stored locally in your browser via IndexedDB and never leave your device.

Third-party services accessed when you use the app

  • Radio Browser API (*.api.radio-browser.info) — when you search for stations, your query is sent to this community-run service. Privacy info.
  • RadioDock Metadata Proxy (radiodock-metadata-proxy-1.onrender.com) — when you play a station, the stream URL is sent to this proxy to fetch "now playing" metadata. The proxy is operated by the same person; source code is open at GitHub. The proxy logs only IP / timestamp / request path for operational diagnostics; logs are not retained long-term.
  • Radio stream servers — your browser connects directly to each station's stream server when you press play. We are not the operator of those servers; refer to each station's own privacy notice.
  • GitHub Pages (host of this site) and Render.com (host of the metadata proxy) record standard server logs (IP address, browser type, timestamp). See GitHub's privacy statement and Render's privacy statement.
  • Buy Me a Coffee — only accessed if you click the "Buy me a coffee" link in the footer. See their privacy policy.

Cookies, analytics, tracking

None. The app does not use cookies, does not run analytics, and does not track you. IndexedDB is used only for your favourites/lists and stays on your device.

Your rights under GDPR / DSGVO

You have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict processing of, and obtain a copy of any personal data held about you, as well as to object to processing and to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. Because the RadioDock app itself holds no personal data, these requests apply to the third-party services listed above; please contact them directly.
